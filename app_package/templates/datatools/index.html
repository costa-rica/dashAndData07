Data Tools

  • NHTSA Recalls and Investigations Dashboard

    National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA) shares all recalls and investigation of US automotive manufactures vehicles. This application stores all records from NHTSA in a database and allows users to add notes, files, link records and add/remove records through a dashboard created specifically for this data.

    This is version is a demo similar to the one used by Kinetic Metrics. There is no proprietary data.

  • What Sticks Health

    What-Sticks-Health is a personal data storage platform that provides feedback to the user. The goal of what-sticks is to provide crude, yet empirically driven inferences on your tendencies. As its name references we are throwing the spaghetti plate of data against the wall and seeing what sticks. Currently, it collects Apple Health Data and Oura Ring device ... more coming!
